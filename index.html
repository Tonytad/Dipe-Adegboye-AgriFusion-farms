Who We Are

Rooted in the Soil,
Growing for Nigeria

Dipe Adegboye AgriFusion Farms is a fully registered Nigerian agricultural enterprise dedicated to sustainable cultivation of palm trees, palm kernel, cocoa, and cashew. We operate a full production cycle from nursery seedling to refined product delivery.

  • ✅ Palm tree cultivation and palm kernel production on large-scale plantations
  • ✅ Premium palm oil refined and packaged in kegs and drums for bulk buyers
  • ✅ Cashew nut farming from planting through processing and export-ready packaging
  • ✅ Cocoa beans grown and harvested to international quality standards
  • ✅ In-house nursery producing cocoa and palm kernel seedlings for sale
🏢 Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), Nigeria
What We Produce

Our Agricultural Products

A diverse portfolio of high-value agricultural produce cultivated across our expansive farmlands with best-in-class farming practices.

🌴
Palm Tree Cultivation

Large-scale oil palm plantation management producing fresh fruit bunches (FFB). Our trees are maintained for maximum yield per hectare, contributing to Nigeria's agricultural output.

🥜
Palm Kernel

Premium quality palm kernel extracted using modern processing methods. Sold in bulk to industrial buyers, soap manufacturers, and commodity traders across Nigeria and West Africa.

🌰
Cashew

Our cashew orchards yield high-quality raw cashew nuts (RCN) harvested seasonally. Sold to local processors and export merchants looking for premium Nigerian cashew.

🍫
Cocoa

Quality-grade cocoa beans grown and fermented on-farm, meeting export and domestic processing standards. We supply to chocolate manufacturers, cocoa processors, and commodity exporters.

Nursery Services

Seedlings for
Tomorrow's Harvest

Our on-site nursery is one of the most comprehensive in the region, producing high-germination, disease-resistant seedlings that give farmers the best possible start.

  • 🌱Cocoa Seedlings — Hybrid varieties selected for high yield and disease resistance. Ready for transplanting at the optimal growth stage.
  • 🌱Palm Kernel Seedlings — Tenera-variety palm seedlings verified for superior fruit quality and oil extraction rates.
  • 🌱Bulk orders available for large-scale plantation establishment projects
  • 🌱Agricultural advisory support included with large seedling orders

Order Seedlings Today

Contact us to discuss bulk seedling orders, pricing, and delivery arrangements. We supply to individual farmers, cooperatives, and agro-investment projects.
